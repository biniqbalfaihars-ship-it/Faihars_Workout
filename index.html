Progression Rules

  • Train each working set with 1–2 reps in reserve (RIR).
  • Take the final set of each exercise close to failure.
  • Increase the weight by 2.5–5 kg once you hit the top of the rep range.
  • Can't add weight? Add 1–2 more reps than last week instead.
  • Rest 2–3 minutes on compound lifts.
  • Rest 60–90 seconds on isolation lifts.

Nutrition Goals

  • Protein: 130–150 g / day
  • Water: 3–4 L / day
  • Calorie surplus: +250 to +350 kcal / day
  • Sleep: 7.5–9 hours every night
